ARCHIPELAGO

Associates Limited

Archipelago Associates Limited (AAL) is an InsureTech enabler specialist offering unique tech expertise to incumbent insurers, as well as to other new-age firms across Asia. Since our inception, we have generated significant interest from insurers who share their trust in our commitment for progress, innovation and determination for the best insurance experience.

THE FOUNDATION

Business Model Innovation: Understanding that the heart of it all is on leveraging digital capabilities to expand our core or to rethink the entire business model.
Agile Organisations: Drive speed and nimbleness in the organisational / operating model and business outcomes to inspire innovation and shape passionate and purpose-driven co-founders who can incubate and build ideas using lean startup principles.
Customer-Centric: Deep understanding of pain points and coupling it with digital solutions to bring new possibilities to life.
Data & Insights: Enables AAL to make informed decisions, simultaneously build individualised solutions for consumers.
